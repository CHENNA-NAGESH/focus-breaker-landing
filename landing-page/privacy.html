Privacy Policy for Focus Breaker

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy details how **Focus Breaker** operates and confirms our commitment to keeping your data secure and private.

1. 100% Local Processing & Offline Storage

Focus Breaker is designed to operate entirely locally within your Google Chrome browser context. It runs completely offline without sending any data over the internet.

  • **No External Server Communication**: Your configurations, active timers, and list of blocked websites never leave your computer.
  • **Local Storage Only**: All user configurations and domain lists are saved exclusively in your browser's secure local storage (`chrome.storage.local`).

2. Data Collection & Transmission

We do not collect, store, track, sell, or transmit any user data. Specifically:

  • We do **not** collect personal identification details (name, email, etc.).
  • We do **not** track your browsing history or compile web analytics.
  • We do **not** use cookies or trackers.

3. Explanation of Browser Permissions

To perform its single function of blocking distracting websites, the extension requests the following permissions:

  • storage: Required to save and persist your focus sessions and block lists on your computer.
  • alarms: Required to track session durations in the background so you are redirected accurately when your focus time ends.
  • tabs: Required to check the URL of the active browser tab when you click enable to see if a blocked site is running.
  • declarativeNetRequest: Required to intercept navigation to restricted domains and redirect you safely to the local blocked breathing page.
  • notifications: Required to alert you with a desktop notification when a focus block timer has successfully completed.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via our official homepage.
